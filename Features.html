2.Why 2048 words?

There are exactly 2048 words in the BIP‚Äë39 list, because with 11 bits you get every possible combination of 0 and 1. Each combination maps to a unique word.

Think of it like the martingale matingale in gambling:

  • 2 bits ‚Üí 4 possibilities
  • 3 bits ‚Üí 8 possibilities
  • 4 bits ‚Üí 16 possibilities

üëâ Just like in a casino: every time you add a bit, the number of combinations doubles. That‚Äôs why the list has 2048 words: one word for every possible combination, with none missing.

3. From Entropy to Words

To create a 12‚Äëword key, remember that each word represents 11 boxes, each filled with either a 0 or a 1 ‚Äî that‚Äôs 132 boxes (bits) in total.

Imagine you sit at your computer and press two keys at random: the 0 key and the 1 key, exactly 128 times.

üëâ Result: you get a long sequence of 128 digits made only of 0 and 1. This is what we call entropy

Then, this sequence is verified:

  • A special calculation called SHA‚Äë256 is applied.
  • We take the first 4 digits of the result (which are also just 0 and 1). These 4 digits are called the checksum
  • We add them at the end, making a total of 132 digits.

We then split this sequence into 12 groups of 11 digits. Each group corresponds exactly to a word in the official list of 2048 words.

üëâ Bottom line: When you read a 12‚Äëword seed phrase, you are actually reading 132 keystrokes of 0 and 1 on an invisible keyboard.

Sovereign Seed Generator

The Sovereign Seed Generator project was designed to give users back a share of control in creating their keys. Unlike the traditional process where everything is generated automatically,

you choose your first 5 words from the official BIP‚Äë39 list. These 5 words already represent 55 bits

the equivalent of 55 keystrokes of 0 and 1 on an invisible keyboard. From there, our generator automatically completes the remaining words to reach the required 128 bits, computes the checksum using SHA‚Äë256, and delivers a complete 12‚Äëword phrase valid in any wallet.

way, you keep personal control over part of your seed, while the system ensures the validity and security of the rest, resulting in a universally recognized key that carries your own personal touch.
